How To Play

Goal

Earn as many points as possible. Books are the most valuable, as always! Destroy anything and everything that gets in your way. Don't get killed in the process!

Controls

↑ (The up arrow)
Moves Montag up.

↓ (The down arrow)
Moves Montag down.

← (The left arrow)
Moves Montag left. Cheat code: "endgame"

→ (The right arrow)
Moves Montag right.

The spacebar
Makes Montag shoot fireballs, if he has any kerosene.

The return key
Uses the input cheat code.

What Means What

Entities

Montag (You)
Montag has 100 health points, and shoots fireballs. Cheat code: "wave"

Firemen
Firemen have an 80% chance of spawning, 5 health points, worth 10 points, and by far the slowest enemy in the game.

Beetle
Beetles have a 15% chance of spawning, 30 health points, worth 20 points, and by far the fastest enemy in the game.

Hound
Hounds have a 5% chance of spawning, 80 health points, and are worth 40 points.
