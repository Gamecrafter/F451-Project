How To Play

Goal

Earn as many points as possible. Books are the most valuable, as always! Destroy anything and everything that gets in your way.

Controls

↑ (The up arrow)

Moves Montag up.

↓ (The down arrow)

Moves Montag down.

← (The left arrow)

Moves Montag left.

→ (The right arrow)

Moves Montag right.

The spacebar

Makes Montag shoot fireballs, if he has any kerosene.
